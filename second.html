BreadOmelette

About BreadOmelette

Quick and easy breakfast sandwich made with eggs, bread, veggies, spices, cheese and butter.

Ingredients

  • For the dosa batter:
    • 2 Whole Eggs
    • 2 tbsp Chopped Onions
    • 2 tbsp Chopped Bell Pepper (any color bell pepper would work)
    • 1 tbsp Chopped Cilantro
    • 1 tsp Chopped Green Chili
