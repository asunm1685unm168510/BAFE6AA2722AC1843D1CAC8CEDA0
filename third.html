About chicken sanwitch

Ingredients

  • 180 grams (6.5 ounces) boneless chicken ((breast/thigh/any or 1 cup (4 ounces, 140 grams) shredded roast/boiled chicken))
  • 1 tablespoon oil ((+ 1 tbsp butter optional))
  • 1½ to 2 tablespoons mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt (start with lower amounts))
  • 1 to 1½ tablespoons sour cream (or Greek yogurt or cream cheese)
  • ¼ teaspoon dry mustard ((or ¼ tablespoon Dijon mustard))
